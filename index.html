Who We Are

Our Mission

GRC bridges the gap between academic theory and real-world impact. We equip Emory students with the skills, network, and experience to tackle complex global challenges.

Founded at Emory University, GRC is dedicated to fostering the next generation of global thinkers and problem-solvers. Through rigorous research, collaborative consulting engagements, and continuous professional development, our members gain hands-on experience working with clients across sectors and geographies.

Global Perspective

We approach every challenge with an international lens, drawing on diverse viewpoints and cross-cultural expertise.

Rigorous Research

Our deliverables are backed by thorough data analysis, literature review, and structured frameworks.

Collaborative Culture

We believe the best solutions emerge from teams that value every voice, background, and discipline.

Tangible Impact

Our work produces actionable recommendations that clients can implement — real change, not theoretical exercises.
